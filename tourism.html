Business Case: Transportation and Tourism

Tourism Management

In managing efficient tourism solutions, algorithms play a vital role in ensuring seamless operations and optimal resource utilization. For tourism, we utilize:

  • BFS (Breadth-First Search): BFS is used for exploring various travel routes and connections within tourist destinations, ensuring that eco-friendly tourism services like e-bike rentals are accessible and efficiently managed.

Transportation Infrastructure

For transportation infrastructure, the following algorithm is used:

  • Kruskal’s Algorithm: Kruskal’s algorithm helps manage transportation networks such as roadways, bridges, and public transit hubs. It optimizes cost-effectiveness and ensures robust connectivity across different transportation modes.
