Business Case: Transportation and Tourism

Use of Algorithms

In this business case focusing on transportation and tourism, we utilized two key algorithms:

BFS (Breadth-First Search)

Feature Details
Purpose Managing efficient travel solutions, especially when exploring connections and routes in a graph-like system.
Use Case Eco-friendly transport solutions like e-bike services and shortest path calculations.
Advantages Ensures optimal route exploration and minimal travel costs.

Kruskal's Algorithm

Feature Details
Purpose Managing transportation infrastructure like road networks, bridges, and public transit hubs.
Use Case Minimizing infrastructure costs while ensuring optimal connectivity and reliability.
Advantages Efficient for building minimum spanning trees, reducing redundancy in networks.
