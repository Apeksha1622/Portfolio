Business Case: Transportation and Tourism

Use of Algorithms

In this business case focusing on transportation and tourism, we utilized two key algorithms:

  • BFS (Breadth-First Search): This algorithm is used for managing efficient travel solutions, especially when exploring connections and routes in a graph-like system. BFS is optimal for scenarios where the shortest path or widest possible exploration is required, ensuring eco-friendly transport solutions like e-bike services are seamlessly integrated.
  • Kruskal’s Algorithm: Kruskal’s algorithm is employed for managing transportation infrastructure such as efficient road networks, bridges, and public transit hubs. It helps minimize costs while ensuring optimal connectivity and infrastructure reliability in tourism management.
