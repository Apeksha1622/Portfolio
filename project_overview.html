Project Overview

ಕಾವೇರಿ ವನ (Kaveri Vana)

Welcome to Smart City Kaveri Vana (ಕಾವೇರಿ ವನ)

Introduction: Kaveri Vana (ಕಾವೇರಿ ವನ) is a new smart city being planned over an area of 1500 square kilometers. The goal is to create a modern, efficient, and sustainable city that uses advanced technology to improve the quality of life for its residents. With innovative infrastructure, smart services, and eco-friendly designs, Kaveri Vana aims to become a model for future urban development.
